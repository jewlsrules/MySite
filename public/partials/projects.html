Projects

Hook & Needle

Hook & Needle is an web app built with Node, Express, Bootstrap, & Mongoose and hosted on Heroku. It is an online community for knitters and crocheters where they can add project insperation, track their progress, and view other members' projects.

SongBird

SongBird is a single page app built with Angular, Node, Express, & Mongoose and hosted on Heroku with data hosted on MongoDB Atlas. Logged in users can create and edit collections of music videos hosted on YouTube. This was created with Ayla Ex & Becca King.
